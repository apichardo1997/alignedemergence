Hey, I'm Anabel

After spending years in finance watching algorithms reshape markets, I became fascinated by a bigger question: What happens when machines don't just process our data, but start to think like us?

I have a background in finance, a deep curiosity about economics and technology, and a brain that won't stop chasing the implications of artificial intelligence. This blog is where I explore the messy, complex, and utterly human questions that AI raises.

My Mission

I believe we can grow with AI—but only if we approach it thoughtfully. My goal is to make the biggest questions about our technological future accessible to everyone, not just engineers and CEOs.

I'm not here to predict the future or claim expertise. I'm here to explore the questions that keep me up at night: Is AI humanity's next great leap forward, or are we playing with fire? How do we maintain our humanity as machines become more human-like?

What to Expect

I publish new explorations weekly, diving into one question or connecting unexpected dots between seemingly unrelated ideas. Recent posts have explored everything from whether AI can be creative to how ancient philosophy applies to modern algorithms.

My writing style is conversational and questioning—I use AI to help with structure and grammar, but every idea and voice you encounter is genuinely human. If something ever sounds robotic, call me out. I'm learning too.

"If you're confused about how these topics connect... good. That means you're not a large language model. You're a human. Just like me."

A Few Ground Rules

I'm not here to be an expert or die on theoretical hills. If you disagree with something I write—connect with me! Enlighten me! That's the whole point.

Everything I write comes from a place of respect, whether I'm touching on religion, gender, mental health, or controversial tech topics. These are my opinions, shaped by my lens, and I hope yours are rooted in critical thinking too. Let's trade lenses sometimes.
